Accomodation & Fest Pass




Kaltarang '24 is a 3-day long cultural fest, and we understand that you will need a place to stay. We have made arrangements for the same, at the cheapest price possible. You can find the details below.

Kaltarang '24 comes with two types of options. You can either opt for the Fest Pass or the Fest Pass with Accomodation.

1. Fest Pass (Only) will give you access to all the events and competitions along with our thrilling pro nights.

2. Fest Pass with Accomodation will give you access to all the events, competitions and our thrilling pro nights along with accommodation facilities (including food, water and shelter).

Following are the charges for the same (inclusive of all taxes):
S. No. Options Price (in Rs)
1 Fest Pass (Only) 500
2 Fest Pass with Accomodation 1000

About Kaltarang

Kaltarang, the lively cultural event at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, shines bright with students' creative spirit. It's a showcase of their hard work, captivating everyone with its beauty and sparking curiosity. This yearly fest brings people together through various art forms, nurturing future cultural leaders. With careful planning, students collaborate, refining their talents and leadership skills. With each passing year, Kaltarang has etched its legacy, hosting renowned artists like Gajendra Verma, Aditya Narain, and Nikhil D'Souza, whose magical performances leave the audience spellbound. Kaltarang is a symbol of cultural greatness, inspiring all to embrace the power of art.

About RGIPT

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is a premier scientific and management training, educational, and research institution established by the Government of India in 2008. The Institute has been accorded the status of "An Institution of National Importance" along the lines of IIT under an act of Parliament (Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Act 2007). The horizons of this institute stretch from the Energy to the IT sector making it one of the best institutes this country has seen. RGIPT aims to provide excellence in education with character building to all the budding enthusiasts in and out of the institute

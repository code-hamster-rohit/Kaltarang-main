FAQs

Q. How do I reach Rajiv Gandhi Institute Of Petroleum Technology?

Ans. Rajiv Gandhi Institute Of Petroleum technology is situated in Jais, district Amethi, Uttar Pradesh which is approximately 100 KM south east from Lucknow. It is well connected by rail and roads.The nearest airport is Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport-Amausi ,Lucknow whereas the nearest station is Jais which is just 1 km from Rgipt campus.
Buses ply regularly from Lucknow as well as Raebareli and Allahabad.


Q. What is the duration of KALTARANG 2024?

Ans. Kaltarang 2024 is a three day offline event from 12th April 2024 to 14th of April 2024 at RGIPT campus which shall host power packed performances from participants across colleges and cultures with a footfall of more than 1000 students.


Q. Will I get accommodations for KALTARANG 2024 ?

Ans. Yes you shall be provided with best available accommodation facilities at Rajiv Gandhi Institute Of Petroleum Institute.
For more details about accommodation facilities, visit the accommodation page.


Q. Will I have the mess facility at the campus during the fest ?

Ans. Yes, you shall be provided with the mess facility during your stay for the cultural event.


Q. Are there any restrictions on movement in the campus at night?

Ans. No, there is no such restriction on movement inside the campus during any part of the day.You are free to explore the campus,we have vigilling security services.


Q. Whom to contact for problems faced if any,during the stay for KALTARANG 2024?

Ans. You can easily reach out to the members of the Cultural Council to report any grievances or problems faced during your stay. We will try to provide you with the best stay experiences.


You can also reach out to us at our contact page for any queries or problems faced.
